==============
PrintEphemeris
==============

Print geometric positions and velocities in J2000 relative to the Solar System Barycenter 
for solar system bodies.

*****
Usage
*****

.. include:: ../toolDescriptions/PrintEphemeris.txt
    :literal:

*******
Example
*******

Print a table of positions and velocities at 2025 November 30 00:00:00 UTC

::

    ./KEPPLR-2025.11.30/scripts/PrintEphemeris -config KEPPLR.config -date 2025 Nov 30
